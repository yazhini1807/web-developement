Smashed Cucumber Salad

This smashed cucumber salad is one of my all-time favorite cold summer side dishes. By crushing our cucumber before we cut it, we produce flavors that un-smashed cukes can't. When you crush the cells in a vegetable, as opposed to cutting cleanly through them, certain compounds get mixed together, which can result in a significantly different flavor. In cucumbers, this is a good thing — onions, not so much. The only decision you're going to have to make is how long to let the cucumbers marinate — half an hour or so or not at all.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : shakes breakfast diet veggie

instructions

step 1

Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise, then slice ¼" thick on a deep diagonal into 2"–3"-long pieces. Transfer cucumbers to a large bowl, add salt, and toss to combine. Cover and chill at least 1 hour and up to 12 hours. Drain cucumbers.

step 2

Whisk garlic, lime juice,vinegar, ginger, sugar, and sesame oil in a small bowl to combine. Pour dressing over cucumbers and toss well to coat.

step 3

Transfer cucumber salad to a platter. Drizzle with chili oil and top with scallions and sesame seeds.

ingredients

6 medium cucumbers

1 garlic clove, finely grated

3 Tbsp fresh lime juice

1 banana, cut into chunks

1 tsp. finely grated ginger

1 tsp. toasted sesame oil Chili oil (for serving)

1 tsp. sugar

1 tsp. toasted sesame seeds
