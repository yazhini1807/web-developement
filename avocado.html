Avocado Milkshake

It’s a healthy and creamy shake made from only 3 ingredients – ripe avocado, milk and sugar. Avocado, which is also known as Butter Fruit, is rich in Vitamin K, B5, B6, C, E, Potassium, Magnesium, fiber, healthy fat and has high amount of good cholesterol which is good for heart. Avocado flesh has creamy texture and hence, its milkshake doesn’t need ice cream to bring creaminess. Use non-dairy milkinstead of regular milk to make Vegan Avocado Shake.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

2 min.

serving

2servings

Tags : shakes breakfast diet veggie

instructions

step 1

Choose ripe avocado by pressing it gently; if it is hard, it means Avocado is unripe; if it’s too soft then it means Avocado is overripe; if it is soft yet little firm, then it means Avocado is ready. Cut it into half and remove the pit.

step 2

Scoop the pulp out using a spoon and place it into a blender jar. Add milk and honey.

step 3

Blend until smooth texture.

ingredients

1 Cup milk

1 ripe avocado, peeled and pitted

3 tablespoons honey

1 banana, cut into chunks
